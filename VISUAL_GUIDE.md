- **Secondary Green** (#10B981) - Success, positive actions
- **Accent Amber** (#F59E0B) - Warnings, recommendations
- **Gray** (various shades) - Text, backgrounds
- **White** - Cards, containers

### Typography
- **Headings** - Bold, large
- **Body Text** - Regular, readable
- **Buttons** - Semibold, clear CTAs
- **Labels** - Small, muted

### Components
- **Cards** - Rounded corners, subtle shadows
- **Buttons** - Rounded, smooth hover effects
- **Forms** - Clean inputs with borders
